What is FDOB?

First Day of Business, or FDOB, is a new central resource designed to help LCS sellers accelerate their planning for the upcoming quarter. This hub serves as the source of truth for all the essential go-to-market materials you need to win, including global product strategy, sales behaviors, and training. The material is refreshed every quarter.

Who is the primary target audience for FDOB?

FDOB is tailored for LCS frontline sellers. This strategic launchpad provides the specific training and product insights you need to drive results in the months ahead.

How should this resource be used?

FDOB helps you move from reactive administrative tasks to proactive, field-ready execution so you can start driving revenue on day one of the quarter. This site is updated every three months — and the Newsfeed section every week — so you can continuously revisit it for the latest material.

Are these materials externally shareable?

This site is an internal resource designed specifically for LCS sellers. While strategic materials like battlecards, workflow training, and incentive details can't be shared, the Resources page provides prep-and-pitch collateral, pitch decks, and external guides that are specifically designed for downloading and sharing with your customers.

How often will this site be updated?

The site's Newsfeed section will be updated every week with critical updates on products and tools, pulled directly from sources like GPS Weekly and The Stack. All other resources will be updated quarterly.

Does this resource replace other resources I used to check?

The new FDOB site streamlines information previously scattered across fragmented emails and siloed hubs. This consolidation aims to significantly reduce your administrative search time, allowing you to stop scrolling and start scoring.

What's the connection between FDOB and the Game On initiative?

In 1H 2026, Game On learning was all about building proficiency and muscle memory to establish a strong sales foundation. Now, the emphasis is on proactive, field-ready execution. Think of FDOB as the vehicle that converts your 1H training into 2H revenue. It's designed to carry that Game On energy forward — so you can seamlessly go from game plan to goal line.

What product areas does FDOB cover?

FDOB covers three global product pillars: Search+, YouTube+, and Measurement.
